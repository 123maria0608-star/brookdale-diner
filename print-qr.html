Setup Tips for Patio Event

  • Print on card stock (110 lb / 199 gsm) then laminate — holds up outdoors
  • Use tent-fold holders or stand clips — waterproof acrylic ones from Amazon work great
  • WiFi extenders: place one TP-Link RE315 or similar near the patio entrance — aim for -65 dBm or better signal at farthest table
  • Run a speed test at the farthest patio table before the event — 5 Mbps+ is enough for QR payments
  • Have your Venmo, Cash App, Zelle, and Square accounts open on your phone so you can verify incoming payments instantly
  • Print a backup cheat sheet with all your payment handles for staff (in case customers ask)
  • Collect Square / Tap to Pay backup on your iPhone for Apple Pay and tap-based payments
