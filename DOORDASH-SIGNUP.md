## Step 3 — What you'll need to provide yourself

DoorDash will ask for these — only you can:

- **Owner full legal name + DOB** (for identity verification)
- **Owner SSN or ITIN** (for tax reporting — they 1099 you)
- **Business EIN** (if you have one) or SSN if sole prop
- **Bank routing + account number** (where they deposit your share)
- **Voided check or bank statement** (uploaded as PDF/JPEG)

**Have these ready before you start the form** — you can't save and resume mid-flow without losing data.

## Step 6 — Pricing tiers

DoorDash will offer three commission tiers:

| Tier | Commission | What you get |
|---|---|---|
| Basic | 15% | Marketplace listing only |
| Plus | 25% | Listing + DashPass + ads |
| Premier | 30% | Listing + DashPass + max marketing + delivery guarantee |

**Most diners start on Basic.** You can upgrade later. Don't get talked into Premier on day one.

DoorDash also takes a per-order delivery fee from the customer (you don't pay that), and a $0.30/order processing fee from you.

---

## Step 7 — After you submit

- Activation usually takes **3–7 business days** while they verify your bank info and onboard the menu.
- They'll mail you a **DoorDash tablet** (free) to receive orders. You can also use their iPad app on a personal device.
- You'll get a Merchant Portal login where you can pause the store, change menu/prices, see payouts, and download 1099s.
